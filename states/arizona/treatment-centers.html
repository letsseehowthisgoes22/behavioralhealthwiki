Behavioral Health Treatment in Arizona

Arizona is home to some of the nation's most recognized residential treatment programs, particularly in the Tucson and Wickenburg areas. The state's desert climate and natural environment contribute to a therapeutic setting valued by many in recovery. Arizona facilities offer a wide spectrum of care including substance use treatment, trauma therapy, dual diagnosis programs, and adolescent services. Many programs serve patients from across the country.
