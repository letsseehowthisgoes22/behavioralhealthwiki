Behavioral Health Treatment in California

California has one of the largest and most diverse behavioral health treatment systems in the nation. From Malibu to San Diego, the state offers hundreds of residential, outpatient, and specialty treatment programs. California is known for its adolescent-specific treatment centers, luxury rehabilitation facilities, and innovative approaches to dual diagnosis care. The state's mild climate and varied landscapes provide therapeutic environments across coastal, mountain, and urban settings.
