Behavioral Health Treatment in Texas

Texas has a large and diverse behavioral health treatment system, with facilities concentrated in the Houston, Austin, Dallas-Fort Worth, and San Antonio metropolitan areas. The state offers residential treatment, outpatient programs, and specialized services for substance use disorders, mental health conditions, and adolescent behavioral health needs. Texas treatment centers serve a wide geographic region across the South and Southwest.
