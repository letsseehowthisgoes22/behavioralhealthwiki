Behavioral Health Treatment in Ohio

Ohio's behavioral health treatment system has expanded significantly in response to the opioid crisis and growing mental health needs. The state offers a wide range of residential, outpatient, and community-based treatment programs. Ohio treatment centers serve individuals and families dealing with substance use disorders, co-occurring conditions, and trauma-related needs across urban and rural areas.
