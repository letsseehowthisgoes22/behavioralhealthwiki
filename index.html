Behavioral Health Wiki

A comprehensive, citation-quality reference on addiction medicine, behavioral health, and evidence-based treatment — written for professionals, families, and informed readers.

  • Conditions & Symptoms

    Substance use disorders, co-occurring mental health conditions, behavioral addictions, and their neurobiological basis — including the neuroscience of addiction, reward circuitry, and neuroadaptation.

  • Levels of Care & Treatment Types

    The full continuum of behavioral health treatment — from detoxification and residential treatment through outpatient programs. ASAM Criteria, evidence-based therapeutic modalities, and harm reduction approaches.

  • Find Treatment & Programs

    State-by-state guides to behavioral health treatment programs — residential treatment centers, wilderness therapy, therapeutic boarding schools, and how to evaluate and choose the right program.

  • Insurance, Costs & Rights

    Navigating insurance coverage for behavioral health treatment — parity laws, appeals processes, out-of-network benefits, financial planning, and patient rights under federal and state law.

  • Parents & Family

    Guidance for families navigating a loved one's behavioral health challenges — recognizing warning signs, family therapy approaches, intervention planning, and supporting long-term recovery.

  • School & Education

    Educational rights, school-based mental health services, IEP and 504 accommodations, therapeutic schools, and re-enrollment after treatment — supporting students with behavioral health needs.

  • Medications & Pharmacotherapy

    Medication-assisted treatment for substance use disorders — approved pharmacological agents for alcohol, opioid, and nicotine dependence, mechanisms of action, efficacy data, and clinical protocols.

  • Laws, Privacy & Safety

    Legal frameworks governing behavioral health treatment — 42 CFR Part 2, HIPAA, state licensing requirements, patient safety regulations, involuntary commitment laws, and confidentiality protections.

  • Dictionary / Key Concepts

    Definitions and explanations of essential terms in addiction medicine and behavioral health — clinical terminology, treatment modalities, regulatory concepts, and assessment frameworks.
